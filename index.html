Circus Nocturna

It says we appeared out of nowhere—one fog-drenched evening, on the outskirts of a town that’s since disappeared from maps.
Circus Nocturna doesn’t follow the rules of time or place. We simply arrive. No one sees the crew build it, yet there we stand by dawn, our towering tents and twisting pathways shrouded in a veil of eerie silence.

Our founder, known only as “The Master,” is a figure of legend and whispers. No one knows who—or what—they are. Even us, performers, who are as strange as we are skilled, never have seen The Master. Yet orders are issued, performances arranged, and shadows seem to shift and obey a presence unseen.
Some believe The Master walks among the visitors, hidden in plain sight. Rumor has it that they masquerade as one of the lifelike marionettes of the Marionette Ruler, their hollow eyes following you wherever you go.

But beware—payment here isn’t always made in coins. Some visitors leave with lighter pockets, others with heavier hearts, and a few… don’t leave at all. After all, it’s not just a circus. It’s an invitation to step beyond the veil and into a world where the line between performer and prey is razor-thin.

The Bone Collector’s Lair

A macabre maze where visitors navigate through tunnels lined with skeletal sculptures, while actors portraying undead creatures stalk the unwary. Dare to enter the Bone Collector’s Lair, and you may leave with a piece of yourself missing.

Labyrinth of Shadows

A dark maze filled with mirrors and shifting lights, where ominous laughter and whispers guide or mislead participants. Wander the Labyrinth of Shadows, and the exit may not lead back to the world you know.

The Carnivorous Carousel

A thrilling ride where the horses have a mind of their own, accompanied by eerie music and unexpected jumpscares. Brave the Carnivorous Carousel, where the horses whisper secrets too terrible to repeat.

The Marionette Ruler

A creepy puppet show where life-sized marionettes seem to move on their own, blurring the line between inanimate and alive.
